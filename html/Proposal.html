A. In 1971 Texas Instruments(TI) introduced the 74LS__ series of Integrated Circuits(ICs).

B. In 1975 MOS Technology introduced the 6502 8-bit microprocessor.

C. In 1977, based on these two technologies, Companies like Apple, Radio Shack, Commodore introduced the first Personal Computers. The magazine Byte introduced them as:
"1977 trinity": Commodore PET, Apple II, and TRS-80 Model I

  1. Form a Company that will:
    1. Simulate Commodore, Apple and Radio Shack
    2. Design and Build an 8-bit Computer from the 74LS__ series of Integrated Circuits(ICs).
    3. NOTE: This predates 1975 so this 8-bit Computer will not have the capabilities of the Apple II, TRS-80, or PET; however, the fundamental operations of the computer will be very similar.
