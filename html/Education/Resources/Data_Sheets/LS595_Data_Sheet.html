The 74HC595 is an 8-bit serial-in/serial or parallel-out shift register with a storage register and 3-state outputs. Both the shift and storage register have separate clocks.

Device features:

  1. Serial Input control: DS
  2. Serial Output control: Q7S
  3. Asynchronous reset: !MR
    A LOW on !MR resets the shift register.
  4. Shifting Data:
    1. Data is shifted on Low to High transition of SHCP
    2. Data in Shift Register is shifted on Low to High transition of STCP
  5. Output Enable:
    1. !OE (active Low)
    2. A High on !OE outputs assume a high-impedance OFF-state
    3. Note: !OE control does NOT affect the state of the registers
