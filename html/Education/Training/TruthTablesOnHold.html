The Input Bits are like and odometer: the right most bit(Bit#1) changes the quickest while the left most bit(Bit#4) changes the slowest. If Bit#1 changes at a rate of 8 per sec, Bit#2 will change at a rate 4 per sec, Bit#3 at 2 per sec while Bit#4 at 1 per sec. The GIF below illustrates this. Click on "Play" to see a slower version.

One final modification to Truth Tables that you will probably never see elsewhere but hopefully will find useful as you get into the heart of this project: Gates/ICs and Design. The Outputs depends upon the Inputs. For Morse Code, if the "dots" and "dashes" are the Inputs, the Outputs ("Letters") are dependent upon the Inputs.

In "Digital Electronics" the Outputs (0s and 1s) will depend upon the Inputs (0s and 1s).
