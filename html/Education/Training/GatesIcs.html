There are three more Basic Logic Gates: AND, OR and XOR. XOR is pronounced as Exclusive OR and means: One or the other but "NOT BOTH". The Negative of these Gates are:NAND, NOR, NXOR: NAND == NOT(AND), NOR == NOT(OR), NXOR == NOT(XOR). The following table will allow you to practice with these 6 Gates.
